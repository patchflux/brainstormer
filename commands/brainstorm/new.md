description: Start a new brainstorming session with research and clarification

<command>
Execute the workflow defined in `workflows/new.md`.

This command:
1. Captures your raw idea
2. Runs 4 parallel researchers (market, competitors, willingness, timing)
3. Synthesizes research findings
4. Enters devil's advocate clarification mode
5. Ends when idea is clear, pivoted, or killed

**Usage:**
```
/brainstorm:new
```

Then describe your idea when prompted.
</command>
